PURE INDIA APP

IMPLEMENTED FOR TECHNOVANZA 2016 CONDUCTED BY TCS AND VJTI, MUMBAI

Pure India App is an android app that could be used by the public to report about pollution
problems directly to the authrorites. The complaints could be monitored by the authorites and
appropriate actions could be taken.

Folder Details

 1. Database Details - Include PHP code at the backend
 2. Screenshots - Screenshots of the app and database
 3. Images - Vectors and Splash screens used for the App
 4. app - source codes
